## Description (max 4,000 chars)

```
Folio is the invoicing app for Australian sole traders and solo
businesses who want a clean, fast tool — without analytics, without
trackers, without subscription lock-in.

KEY FEATURES

• ATO-compliant Tax Invoices — every invoice has the words "Tax
  Invoice", your business name, ABN, issue date, line items with
  GST, and the buyer details required for invoices over $1,000.

• Multi-currency, multi-GST — issue AUD invoices with 10% GST or
  per-line overrides for GST-free items. Non-AUD invoices are treated
  as GST-free exports with the right note on the PDF.

• Discounts that match your accountant's expectations — both per-line
  and whole-invoice, applied proportionally so GST stays correct, with
  the math shown on the PDF.

• Credit notes with their own numbering, atomically issued against the
  original invoice, with a "Net balance" summary on the linked invoice.

• Payment tracking — record partial payments, mark fully paid; the
  status flips automatically and the dashboard reflects outstanding +
  overdue.

• Send by email straight from the app, with up to five attachments per
  message. Or share via your phone's native share sheet.

• Custom invoice numbering — your own prefix and pad width, counter
  reset whenever you need it, atomic so you never get duplicates.

• Line item library — save common services with their default qty,
  price, and GST rate, then insert with one tap.

• Archived view + ATO five-year retention — sent invoices and credit
  notes soft-delete to an archive you can browse anytime.

• Exports — CSV (two files: invoices + line items, RFC-4180 escaped)
  and PDF ZIP, for backups or your accountant.

• PIN + biometric on launch — Face ID, Touch ID, or device PIN.

• Light + dark theme, both built day one. Follows your system or pick
  one explicitly.

PRIVACY

Folio collects no analytics, no telemetry, and no crash reports.
Your data lives in your own Firebase project. The only third-party
service Folio talks to is SendGrid, when you press Send Email — it
sees the recipient address and the email body so it can deliver the
mail, and that's it.

WHO IT'S FOR

Single-user, single-business operators who are GST-registered. If
you've outgrown a Google Sheet or you've had it with subscription
fatigue from heavyweight competitors, Folio is the lightweight
middle ground.

WHO IT'S NOT FOR

Multi-employee firms, agency-style billing, recurring invoices, or
anyone who needs full double-entry accounting. Folio doesn't replace
Xero or MYOB.
```
