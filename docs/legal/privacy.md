# Privacy policy

*Last updated: 2026-05-02*

This Privacy Policy explains how Fernando Frizzo ("we", "us", or "our"),
the developer of **Folio**, collects, uses, and protects your personal
data when you use our mobile application. We are based in Australia and
comply with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy
Principles (APPs).

## 1. Who we are

Developer: Fernando Frizzo
Location: Australia
Contact: [fernandofrizzo@gmail.com](mailto:fernandofrizzo@gmail.com)

## 2. Data we collect

- **Authentication data** — if you link your account to Apple or Google
  sign-in, we receive your name and email address from the provider.
  Anonymous accounts collect no personal data.
- **Business profile** — business name, ABN, address, logo, and payment
  details you enter into the app. This data is stored in your own
  Firebase project.
- **Invoice and client data** — invoices, credit notes, client records,
  and payment history you create in the app.
- **Purchase data** — subscription status managed by RevenueCat. We do
  not store your payment card details.
- **Device identifier** — an anonymous device ID used for crash
  reporting.

## 3. How we use your data

- To provide the invoicing service you signed up for.
- To process your premium subscription via RevenueCat.
- To send invoices on your behalf via SendGrid when you tap Send.
- To improve the app through anonymous crash reports.
- To respond to support requests or legal obligations.

## 4. Third-party services

We share data with the following processors, each operating under their
own privacy policies:

- **Firebase** (authentication & database) —
  [firebase.google.com/support/privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
- **RevenueCat** (subscription management) —
  [revenuecat.com/privacy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)
- **SendGrid** (email delivery, Pro tier only) —
  [twilio.com/legal/privacy](https://www.twilio.com/legal/privacy)

We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

## 5. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete
your account, all personal data (authentication record, business
profile, invoices, clients, and settings) is permanently deleted within
**7 days**. See our [account deletion page](./delete-account) for
details.

## 6. Your rights

Under the Australian Privacy Act, you have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you.
- **Correction** of inaccurate data.
- **Erasure** — you can delete your account by contacting us, or delete
  individual records from within the app at any time.
- **Complain** — if you believe we have breached the APPs, you can
  lodge a complaint with the [Office of the Australian Information
  Commissioner (OAIC)](https://www.oaic.gov.au/).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at
[fernandofrizzo@gmail.com](mailto:fernandofrizzo@gmail.com). We will
respond within 30 days.

## 7. Children's privacy

Folio is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect
personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has
provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it
promptly.

## 8. International data transfers

Some of our third-party processors (Firebase, RevenueCat, SendGrid) may
process data outside Australia. Where this occurs, we take reasonable
steps to ensure your data is protected to a standard comparable to the
Australian Privacy Principles.

## 9. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted
connections (TLS/HTTPS) for all data in transit. Authentication
credentials are managed by Firebase Authentication and are never stored
in plain text.

## 10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be
reflected here with an updated date at the top. Your continued use of
the app after a change counts as acceptance.
