*Last updated: 2026-05-02*

written in plain English so you can actually read them.

## Who Folio is for

Folio is built for solo operators of Australian small businesses who are
GST-registered and bill in AUD (with optional non-AUD export invoices).
If you're a multi-employee firm or you need agency-style features, this
app probably isn't the right fit.

## Your data is yours

Everything you create in Folio lives in your own Firebase project. We
don't have access to it, and you can export or delete it at any time.
See the [privacy policy](./privacy.md) for the details.

## ATO compliance — what Folio does and doesn't promise

Folio's PDF template includes the ATO's mandatory tax-invoice fields
("Tax Invoice", business name + ABN, issue date, buyer name on invoices
≥ $1,000, line items with description / qty / price, GST line). The
underlying math (per-line rounding, mixed-rate breakdown wording) follows
the ATO-accepted Xero/MYOB convention.

Folio is not accounting software, an accountant, or a registered tax
agent. We don't validate your business is correctly registered for GST,
that your line items are accurately classified, or that your prices
include or exclude GST as required. **You are responsible for the
correctness of every invoice you issue.**

If you're unsure whether an invoice complies with your obligations,
consult a registered tax agent.

Don't use Folio to issue invoices for goods or services you can't
legally provide, or to disguise transactions. Don't try to break or
abuse the app — Folio sends emails through SendGrid on your behalf, and
abusing that pipe is a fast way to get your SendGrid account suspended.

## Email sending

configured API key. The email lands at the address you typed. Delivery
is best-effort — Folio cannot guarantee any specific email arrives, and
isn't responsible for emails that bounce, end up in spam, or are
filtered by the recipient's mail server.
