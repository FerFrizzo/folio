# Delete your Folio account

*Folio — the invoicing app for Australian small business*

To request deletion of your Folio account and all associated data, email us at:

**[fernandofrizzo@gmail.com](mailto:fernandofrizzo@gmail.com?subject=Delete%20my%20Folio%20account)**

Use the subject line **"Delete my Folio account"** and include the email address linked to your account (if any). We will action your request within **7 days**.

---

## What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

- Business profile (name, ABN, address, logo)
- All clients, invoices, credit notes, and payment records
- Settings and preferences
- Line item library
- Your Firebase authentication record

## What is retained

We do not retain any of your data after deletion. Because Folio stores data in your own Firebase project, once your authentication record and associated Firestore documents are deleted, nothing remains on our systems.

Note: if you have already exported PDFs or CSV files to your device, those local copies are unaffected — we cannot delete files stored on your own device.

## Partial data deletion (optional)

You can also delete individual records at any time from within the app — invoices, credit notes, clients, and line items can each be deleted independently without deleting your account.
